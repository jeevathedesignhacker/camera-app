Camera Connect To Download Pics

-> Start the Bluetooth remote pair process on your camera.

-> Set your camera to the "Self-timer: 10 sec/remote control" drive mode.

-> Set your camera to the "Bulb" exposure mode.


Press and choose your camera among the shown devices.

Note:

Occasionally you might get pairing issues or reconnection issues, e.g. after camera got asleep. When that happens, I found it helpful to "Delete connection information" on the camera, restart it and go through the pairing process again.
